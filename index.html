ME, U, N BBQ

A blog where I, Drake Burdette, share the best BBQ in KC.

This is a blog about what I consider to be some of the best BBQ joints in Kansas City. All of the reviews and ratings are my opinion. We can choose to disagree, but just know that I am always right! (Except when I am wrong!) Follow me on Twitter.

Hello all! My name is Drake, and I love BBQ. I really enjoy the smokey flavor profile of a nice piece of meat. My favorite pieces of meat include ribs, and burnt ends. (Here is a little side note burnt ends is actually something that is only a KC BBQ thing.) One should deffinatley try burnt ends. Hopefully they will change your life like they did mine.

I am learning the art of slow cooking meat at the moment. Hopefully i will master this skill and become the BBQ connoisseur that I know i want to be deep down in my heart.

Joe's KC

This is my favorite BBQ joint in town. There is a long line to wait in at the orginal Joe's KC, formerly known as Oklahoma Joe's, but it is definatley worth the wait. Their ribs are to die for.

As soon as you hop in line the cashiers yell, "Hi, may I help you?" This is the part where you need to hurry up and make up your mind about what you are going to eat.

LC's BBQ

LC's Barbeque is my second favorite BBQ joint in KC. LC deffinatley knows what he is doing with his seasoning and meats. He makes the best burnt ends in town!!!
